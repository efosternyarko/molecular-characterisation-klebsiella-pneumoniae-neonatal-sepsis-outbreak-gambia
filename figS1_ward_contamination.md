# Supplementary Figure 1 — Sources of Bacterial Contamination

**Caption:** Sources of bacterial contamination identified in the labour and neonatal
wards as identified by conventional microbiology during the outbreak investigation.

---

## How this figure was produced

Supplementary Figure 1 was assembled in **Adobe Illustrator** from conventional
microbiology results and ward floor-plan schematics. It is not reproducible from
a single script.

The underlying contamination data (sample locations, organism identifications,
and culture results from the environmental sampling) are provided in the
supplementary data file:

- **File:** `FileS1_to_FileS10.xlsx`
- **Sheet:** `Supp_File2_Study_isolates` — environmental isolates column `Status == "Environmental"`,
  with sample location and culture result columns
